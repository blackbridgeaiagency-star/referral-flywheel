"True! But Whop's system requires members to manually sign up as affiliates. We automate it - every member gets a link when they join, plus you get leaderboards, tier badges, and competition features that drive way more sharing."

**Response:**
"Fair question! But remember:
1. It's only on referred sales (not all revenue)

3. You keep 90% (vs paying members 10-20% typically)
4. Compare to: Building this yourself (weeks + dev costs) or losing those referrals entirely

**Response:**
"That's exactly why we built it to be automated:

- Tracking happens in the background

### **Objection: "I don't want to pay members"**

## üéØ Qualifying Questions

Before deep diving, qualify if they're a good fit:

1. **"How many members do you have?"**
   - <50: Might be too small (but could be early adopter)
   - 50-500: Perfect fit
   - 500+: Great fit, possibly negotiate custom pricing

2. **"Do members ever share your community with friends?"**
   - Yes: Perfect!
   - No: Dig deeper - why not? Could be opportunity
   - Don't know: This is exactly why they need tracking!

3. **"How do you currently track referrals?"**
   - Manual: We automate this
   - Not at all: Missing out on data
   - Spreadsheet: Error-prone, time-consuming

4. **"What's your monthly pricing?"**
   - Higher price = higher referral value = better fit
   - Lower price = need higher volume

## üéâ Closing Tips

1. **Be consultative, not pushy** - You're solving a problem, not forcing a sale
2. **Lead with curiosity** - Ask questions before pitching
3. **Use their language** - Mirror their terminology and pain points
4. **Show, don't tell** - Demo > Description
5. **Make it easy** - Remove friction from trying it
6. **Follow up consistently** - But not annoyingly (3-day gaps)
7. **Know when to walk away** - Not every community is a fit
8. **Collect feedback** - Even from "no's" - improve your pitch

---

**Remember:** You're helping them tap into growth they're already missing. You're not creating new behavior - you're tracking and amplifying existing word-of-mouth.
